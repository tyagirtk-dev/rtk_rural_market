Shipping & Delivery Policy

Last updated: {{ now().strftime('%d %B %Y') }}

1. Pre-Booking Model

RTK Rural Market operates on a pre-booking basis. The business owner travels to the city only once enough confirmed orders accumulate for a delivery city, meaning delivery windows may take a few days depending on demand.

2. Delivery Charges

A standard delivery charge applies per order below a minimum order value. Orders above the free-delivery threshold shown at checkout are delivered free of charge.

3. Delivery Areas

We currently deliver to select cities as listed in each product's "Delivery City" field. If your city is not listed, please contact us to check upcoming delivery runs.

4. Order Tracking

Track your order status — Pending, Confirmed, Packed, Out for Delivery, or Delivered — from your Order History page. You'll also receive email and in-app notifications as the status changes.

5. Missed Deliveries

Please ensure someone is available at the delivery address on the scheduled date, especially for perishable items. Repeated missed deliveries may result in order cancellation without refund for spoiled perishables.

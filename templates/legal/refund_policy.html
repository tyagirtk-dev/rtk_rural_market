Refund Policy

Last updated: {{ now().strftime('%d %B %Y') }}

1. Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds are provided if an order is cancelled by RTK Rural Market due to stock unavailability, or if a product delivered is damaged, spoiled, or significantly different from what was ordered. Please report issues within 24 hours of delivery.

2. Perishable Goods

As most of our products (milk, curd, paneer, vegetables) are perishable, we are unable to accept returns once delivered unless the item was defective, spoiled on arrival, or incorrect.

3. Refund Process

Approved refunds for UPI payments are credited back to the original payment method within 5-7 business days. For Cash on Delivery orders that are cancelled before dispatch, no charge is made.

4. Coupon Adjustments

If a coupon was applied to a partially refunded order, the discount amount will be recalculated proportionally.

5. Contact for Refunds

To request a refund, please reach out via our Contact page with your order number and details of the issue.
